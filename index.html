3. Permissions & Device Access

To function as a comprehensive PDF toolkit, the App requires specific system permissions. We request only what is strictly necessary for each feature.

Camera Access CAMERA

Why we need it: Required for the Document Scanner feature to capture images of physical documents directly from your camera.

What we do with it:

  • Capture images for the document scanning functionality.
  • Images are processed entirely on-device and are never uploaded to any server.
  • You can choose to deny this permission and still use all other features of the app.
Storage Access WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

Why we need it: Required on devices running Android 9 (API 29) and below to read and save PDF files to your device storage.

What we do with it:

  • Read PDF files that you select for editing or viewing.
  • Save edited or converted PDF files to your chosen location.
Internet Access INTERNET

Why we need it: Required by the Google ML Kit Document Scanner library for initial model download. This is a one-time download when you first use the scan feature.

What we do with it:

  • Download on-device machine learning models for document edge detection (via Google ML Kit).
  • We do not upload any of your documents, images, or personal data to the internet.
Photo Gallery Access READ_MEDIA_IMAGES

Why we need it: Required on Android 13+ devices to access your photo gallery for the "Image to PDF" conversion feature.

What we do with it:

  • Access images you specifically select to convert into PDF documents.
  • We only access the images you choose ‚Äì we do not scan or access your entire gallery.
  • Selected images are processed entirely on-device and are never uploaded anywhere.

5. Third-Party Libraries & Services

We utilize industry-standard open-source libraries to power our features. These libraries run locally within the App environment:

  • PDFBox-Android: Apache-licensed library for PDF generation, modification, merging, splitting, and watermarking. Runs entirely offline.
  • AndroidPdfViewer: For rendering and displaying PDF documents within the app.
  • Glide: For efficient image loading and caching within the app.
  • uCrop: For image cropping functionality before conversion to PDF.
  • ZXing: For QR code generation feature. Runs entirely offline.
  • CameraX: Android Jetpack library for camera functionality in the scanning feature.

Google ML Kit Document Scanner

We use Google's ML Kit Document Scanner for enhanced document scanning. This service:

  • Downloads machine learning models to your device (requires Internet permission for initial download).
  • Processes all document scanning on-device ‚Äì your documents are never sent to Google's servers for processing.
  • For more details, see Google ML Kit Documentation.
