Privacy Policy

App Name: PDF Reader & Tools
Last Updated: January 13, 2026

Overview

Your privacy is paramount. PDF Reader & Tools ("the App") is designed as an offline-first utility. This means that unlike many other free tools, we do not upload your sensitive documents to the cloud for processing.

Zero Data Collection Promise: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, usage data, or document contents. All operations are performed locally on your device's processor.

Permissions & Usage

The App requests only the minimum permissions necessary to function as a document editor and manager.

1. Storage Access

Permission: MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (All Files Access)

Reason: This permission is critical for the App's core functionality. It allows you to:

  • Browse and find PDF files stored anywhere on your device.
  • Modify PDF files (Merge, Split, Watermark) and save the results.
  • Organize your library by renaming or deleting unwanted files.

2. Media Access

Permission: READ_MEDIA_IMAGES

Reason: Required on Android 13+ to allow you to select images from your gallery to convert them into PDF documents.

3. No Other Permissions

To ensure your privacy, the App does NOT request or access:

  • Camera: We do not access your camera hardware.
  • Location: We do not track your location.
  • Contacts: We do not access your contact list.
  • Microphone: We do not record audio.

Data Handling

Local Processing

All file operations involved in features such as Image to PDF, Merge PDF, Split PDF, and PDF Compression are executed using the device's own CPU. Your files never leave your device storage.

Analytics & Advertising

The App contains no third-party tracking code. We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, or advertising SDKs (like AdMob). Your usage patterns remain private to you.

Third-Party Libraries

We use trusted open-source libraries to provide functionality. These libraries operate locally within the App:

  • iText7: For generating and modifying PDF files.
  • Glide: For efficiently loading images from your storage.
  • uCrop: For cropping images before conversion.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly address anyone under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal data, we hold no information about users of any age.
