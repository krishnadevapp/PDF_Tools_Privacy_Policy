Privacy Policy

Last Revised: January 14, 2026

1. Introduction

PDF Reader & Tools ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our application handles your data and outlines your rights.

The core philosophy of our App is Privacy by Design. We are a utility application that processes your documents directly on your device.

Zero Trace Policy: We do not collect, upload, process, or store your personal documents, PDF files, or images on any external server. All document processing happens offline on your own device.

2. Information Collection

A. Personal Data

We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • Account credentials or login information.
  • Location data (GPS or IP-based).

B. Usage Data

We do not track your usage behavior, screen views, or interaction history within the App. We do not use third-party analytics services like Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics.

3. Permissions & Device Access

To function as a comprehensive Document Manager, the App requires specific system permissions. We request only what is strictly necessary.

Full Storage Access MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

Why we need it: This is the core permission for the App. As a PDF Manager, users expect to find, organize, and edit PDF files located anywhere on their device (Downloads, Documents, Internal Storage).

What we do with it:

  • Scan device storage to populate the "My Files" list.
  • Allow you to rename, delete, and move PDF files.
  • Save edited or converted files back to specific user-chosen folders.
Photo Gallery Access READ_MEDIA_IMAGES

Why we need it: Required for the "Image to PDF" feature on Android 13+ devices.

What we do with it: Allows you to select specific images from your gallery to convert them into a single PDF document. We only access images you specifically select.

Legacy Storage READ/WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE

Why we need it: Required for older devices (Android 10 and below) to read and save files.

5. Third-Party Libraries

We utilize industry-standard open-source libraries to power our features. These run locally within the App environment:

  • iText7 / PDFBox: Engines for PDF generation and modification.
  • Glide: For efficient image loading and caching.
  • uCrop: For image cropping functionality.

6. Data Security

Since we do not transmit data to the internet, your documents are as secure as your device itself. We recommend keeping your device OS updated and using device-level security (PIN/Biometrics) to protect your local data.

There is no "backend database" for this App that can be breached, as we do not maintain one.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is a general audience utility tool. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information (which is technically impossible given our zero-collection architecture), please contact us.
